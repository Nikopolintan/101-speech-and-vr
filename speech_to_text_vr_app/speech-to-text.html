How to Use

  1. Click "Start Speaking" to activate your microphone
  2. Speak clearly into your microphone
  3. Your speech will be converted to text in real-time
  4. Click "Stop" when you're finished
  5. Click "Translate" to translate your text to another language
  6. You can edit the resulting text if needed
  7. Use "Clear Text" to start over

Note: This feature requires microphone access and works best in Chrome, Edge, or Safari browsers. Translation requires an internet connection.
